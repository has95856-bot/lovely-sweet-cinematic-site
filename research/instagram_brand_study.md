## Strengths
1. Product variety is clear: trays, mini cakes, cup cakes, cheesecake, printed chocolate, sugar ribbons/stickers.
2. Personal handmade story is strong in bio.
3. Products already have event/customization value.
4. WhatsApp order path exists.
5. Visual colors are distinctive: mint logo + cocoa/cream/yellow product palette.

## Weaknesses / solutions
1. Instagram grid shows many nice products, but the brand hierarchy is unclear for a new visitor.
   - Site solution: categorized product journey: Occasion Trays, Printed Chocolate, Mini Cakes, Cup Cakes, Sugar Ribbons, Stickers.
2. Prices are hidden in highlights, not structured.
   - Site solution: browsable catalog + “طلب عرض سعر” by WhatsApp.
3. Logo is charming but low-resolution from Instagram.
   - Site solution: hero spotlight + vector-clean direction; preserve exact mixer/ribbon/mint identity.
4. Ordering likely depends on DM/WhatsApp back-and-forth.
   - Site solution: guided order form that sends prefilled WhatsApp message: occasion, date, quantity, product type, print name.
5. Product photos have mixed lighting/backgrounds.
   - Site solution: website uses cinematic color grading, cards, and curated product crops; future improvement: standard shoot guide.

## Website concept
Name: `Lovely Sweet — simply elegant, simply delicious`

Cinematic story:
1. Logo emerges like a mint bakery seal.
2. A scroll-driven canvas film moves over a luxurious sweets table.
3. Product families reveal as premium cards.
4. Customization journey explains edible printing / occasion messages.
5. WhatsApp order CTA with a ready-order flow.
